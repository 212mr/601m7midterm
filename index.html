A mosaic is a piece of art or image made from the assemblage of small pieces of colored glass, stone, or other materials. Mosaics have a long history, starting in Mesopotamia in the 3rd millennium BC. Modern mosaics are made by professional artists, street artists, and as a popular craft. Many materials other than traditional stone and ceramic tesserae may be employed, including shells, glass and beads.[1]
